About Us

WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd is a leading recruitment agency in the East African region dedicated to promoting peoples’ lives with significant employment and development opportunities as well as providing various companies with the necessary personnel to help them in making a difference in their industry. WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd started operations in 18th May 2019 as a local human resource consultancy firm under the banner Three Hands Worldwide Service. The entity grew to an established, reliable and globally recognized recruitment agency thus branching further to form the WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd. WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd offers unrivaled services in the field of human resources designed to provide our clients with the much needed competitive advantage. Our commitment to deliver has remained as our greatest key to growth and success. WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd is incorporated in the republic of Kenya under the provisions of CAP 486 of laws of Kenya as a limited corporate. We are also accredited as an employment agency both locally and internationally in line with the provisions of Labor Institutions Act, 2007. Over the years, we have developed invaluable links across the various sectors of business and industry around the world. We have a huge global presence having recruited for Companies in the Middle East especially Saudi Arabia, State of Qatar, the United Arab Emirates among others, Europe and Africa.

Our Vision Our Vision is to become a respected and eminent leader in the delivery of invaluable personnel solutions and unrivaled services that make a difference to our clients. We believe that to be persuasive we must be believable, to be believable we must be credible and to be credible we must be truthful to ourselves, our clients and the world at large. We must only be wise in words but also in deeds. We pledge responsibility of our actions that influence the lives of our customers and fellow workers. We recognize that Desire is the key to motivation, but it’s determination and commitment to an unrelenting pursuit of our goal – a commitment to excellence – that will enable us to attain the success we seek. We are committed to and defined by delivery of excellent services to our clients. Our place in the Economy Kenya through its economic blue print, Vision 2030, intends to create a globally competitive and adaptive human resource base and to raise productivity to international levels. WHRC Recruiting Agency Ltd bridges the gap by providing placement opportunities to our people.
